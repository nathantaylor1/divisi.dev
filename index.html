Hack Attack: Code Corruption

In this puzzle-platformer, you have the ultimate control: to hack the game's source code. Customize your controller through a block-based coding system. Discover new code blocks to discover new attacks, homing bullets, and even flight. Build your army of undead office supplies by reprogramming your enemies to aid you in your escape.

Project 3 in EECS 494 at the University of Michigan. Our group of 5 had 6 weeks to make a game, and this is what we came up with. Our game participated at the EECS 494 Showcase on 4/19/2022.

Blocky Battle Arena

Project 2 in EECS 494 at the University of Michigan was to develope a game by ourselves given a two week window. Blocky Battle Arena is what I came up with. The game centers around a tank in a procedurally generated map. Defeat enemies and collect coins to gain points. Don't let the enemies shoot you back!

Metroid Recreation

Project 1 in EECS 494 at the University of Michigan. Our group of 2 implemented the first area of Metroid for the NES in the Unity Game Engine, added a custom mechanic and built a new level based off of that mechanic.
